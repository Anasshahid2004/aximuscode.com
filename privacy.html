Privacy Policy

At Aximus Code, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our software development services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, Email Address, Phone Number, Company Name, Billing Details.
  • Technical Information: IP address, Browser Type, Operating System, Device Information, and Website Usage Data.
  • Project Information: Details related to your software requirements, specifications, and project discussions.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage our software services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your inquiries, projects, and support.
  • To improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • To send updates, newsletters, or promotional offers (you can opt out anytime).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your personal and project-related data. All communications are encrypted, and sensitive information is securely stored.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your data may only be shared with:

  • Trusted third-party vendors involved in project development (under NDA).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and understand how users interact with our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request data portability.

To exercise your rights, contact us at careers.aximuscode@gmail.com.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
